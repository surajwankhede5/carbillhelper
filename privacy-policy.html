🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Data,
Your Control

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Effective: 1 January 2025  |  Last Updated: March 2026
📋
Section 01
Information We Collect
📱 Data Stored Locally on Your Device
  • Vehicle Details — Car/bike brand, model, variant, fuel type, year, registration number, odometer
  • Service History — Dates, mileage, workshop name, work done, bill amount, notes
  • Documents — RC, Insurance, PUC, Fitness details (text only unless you upload photo)
  • Parts Tracker — Replacement dates, KM, brand, cost per part
  • EMI / Loan Details — Loan amount, tenure, interest rate if entered
📸 Data You Share — Bill Advisory Service Only
  • Name & WhatsApp Number — Only when you submit a Bill Advisory request
  • Bill Photo — Image you choose to upload for review
  • Vehicle Details — Brand, model, KM for advisory context
✅ Most data stays on YOUR device. Vehicle history, service records, documents are stored in your phone's local storage. We do not access this data automatically.
🎯
Section 02
How We Use Your Data
  • App Service — Health scores, service reminders, parts life, expiry alerts
  • Bill Review — Analyze your service bill and provide expert feedback via WhatsApp
  • Insurance Advisory — Personalized recommendations based on your vehicle
  • Customer Support — Respond to your queries via WhatsApp or email
🚫 We will NEVER use your data for: advertising targeting, selling to third parties, spam, or profiling without consent.
🗄️
Section 03
Data Storage & Retention
📱 Local Storage (Your Device)

All vehicle data is stored locally. Deleted when you clear app data or uninstall. Export anytime from Docs → Backup.

☁️ Cloud Storage (Bill Advisory Only)
  • Bill photos uploaded to Cloudinary (encrypted cloud)
  • Submission details saved to Supabase (India-region database)
  • Retained for 90 days then auto-deleted
  • Request deletion anytime via WhatsApp

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data

  • Withdraw Consent — Stop communications anytime
